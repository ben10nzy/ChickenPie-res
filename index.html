Difference in Celestial Bodies

What is the difference between Asteroids, Meteorites, and Comet ?

  • Asteroids is small rock formed from the remnants of the formation of the solar system.
  • Meteoroites come from fragments of asteroids that enter the earth.
  • Comets is celestial body made of ice and dust
